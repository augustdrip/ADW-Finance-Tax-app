## 1. Income Sections (ยง61-91)

### ยง61 - Gross Income Defined
- **Summary**: Defines gross income as all income from whatever source derived
- **Key Points**:
  - Income is broadly defined - if in doubt, it's probably taxable
  - Includes both cash and non-cash compensation
  - Bartering creates taxable income at fair market value
  - Forgiven debt is generally taxable income
- **Business Relevance**: HIGH
- **Forms**: Form 1040, Schedule C, Schedule 1

### ยง62 - Adjusted Gross Income Defined
- **Summary**: Defines AGI as gross income minus specific 'above-the-line' deductions
- **Key Points**:
  - Business expenses are 'above the line' deductions
  - Self-employed health insurance deduction
  - 50% of self-employment tax is deductible
  - SEP, SIMPLE, and qualified plan contributions
- **Business Relevance**: HIGH
- **Forms**: Form 1040, Schedule 1

### ยง63 - Taxable Income Defined
- **Summary**: Taxable income equals AGI minus deductions and QBI deduction
- **Key Points**:
  - Standard deduction 2025: $15,000 single, $30,000 MFJ
  - SALT deduction capped at $10,000
- **Business Relevance**: MEDIUM
- **Forms**: Form 1040, Schedule A

### ยง74 - Prizes and Awards
- **Summary**: Prizes and awards are generally included in gross income
- **Key Points**:
  - Business awards/prizes are taxable income
  - Employee achievement awards excludable up to $1,600
- **Business Relevance**: LOW
